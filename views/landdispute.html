Addressing Land Disputes

Key Issues and Solutions:

  • Strengthening Land Governance

    Implementing reforms to improve land administration systems, update land records, and enhance transparency in land transactions.

  • Legal Reforms

    Advocating for comprehensive legal reforms to clarify land rights, streamline land laws, and address inconsistencies in land legislation.

  • Community Participation

    Promoting community involvement in land management decisions, fostering dialogue, and empowering local communities to resolve disputes.

  • Environmental Conservation

    Integrating environmental concerns into land policies, balancing conservation efforts with sustainable development, and protecting ecologically sensitive areas.

  • Access to Justice

    Improving access to legal aid, establishing specialized land courts, and promoting alternative dispute resolution mechanisms to expedite resolution of land disputes.
